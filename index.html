How To Cook a Steak

Step #1: Season with spices, herbs, and oils including salt, pepper, garlic, thyme, olive oil, etc.

Step #2: Set stove to 400-450 degrees Fahrenheit and put oil on pan

Step #3: Put the steak in a pan and sear evenly on both sides and add more seasoning if desired

Step #4: Flip the steak every 1-2 minutes, sear the edges, and check the inside color occasionally

Step #5: When outside and inside of the steak are to your liking, turn off the stove, take steak off of the pan, add more salt/pepper if desired, and serve
